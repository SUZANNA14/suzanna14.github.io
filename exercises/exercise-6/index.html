Audrey Hepburn

Actress

Audrey Hepburn, the epitome of grace and elegance, takes on the role of Regina Lampert, a young widow caught in a web of deception and intrigue. Known for her iconic style and impeccable acting, Hepburn's performance in Charade showcases her talent as a leading lady in the Golden Age of Hollywood.

Cary Grant

Actor

Cary Grant, the suave and debonair leading man of his era, plays the mysterious and charismatic Peter Joshua. His impeccable comedic timing and effortless charm make him the perfect foil to Hepburn's character, creating a delightful on-screen chemistry that's unforgettable.

Stanley Donen

Director

Stanley Donen, the visionary director behind this film, brings a blend of suspense and humor to the story, creating a cinematic experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat while making you laugh out loud. His direction in Charade is a testament to his filmmaking genius.
